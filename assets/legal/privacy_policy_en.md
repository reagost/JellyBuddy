# JellyBuddy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

---

## Overview

JellyBuddy ("the App") is an AI-powered gamified programming learning application. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

In short: **We do not collect any data.**

---

## Data Storage

All data in this App is stored entirely on your local device, including but not limited to:

- Learning progress and scores
- Experience points, hearts, streak counts, and other gamification data
- Achievements and badges
- Course content and exercise records
- AI conversation history
- App settings and preferences

**This data is never uploaded to any server and is never shared with any third party.**

---

## Data Collection

This App does **not** collect any personal data, including but not limited to:

- No collection of personally identifiable information (name, email, phone, etc.)
- No collection of device information
- No collection of location data
- No collection of usage statistics or analytics data
- No collection of crash reports
- No use of any tracking technologies (cookies, tracking SDKs, etc.)

---

## User Accounts

This App does **not** require user registration or login of any kind. There is no user account system and no cloud sync functionality.

---

## AI Models

The AI large language models used by this App run entirely on your local device:

- AI inference does not require an internet connection
- Your conversations with the AI are never sent to any server
- AI model files are downloaded from public open-source repositories; once downloaded, they run completely offline
- Model download is the only feature that requires an internet connection

---

## Third-Party SDKs

This App does **not** include any third-party SDKs that collect user data, including but not limited to:

- No advertising SDKs
- No analytics SDKs (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- No social media SDKs
- No push notification services (except system-level local notifications)
- No crash reporting SDKs

---

## Network Communication

This App uses a network connection only in the following case:

- Initial download of AI model files (from public open-source model repositories)

Beyond this, the App operates entirely offline and does not make any network requests.

---

## Children's Privacy

This App is suitable for users of all ages (4+). Since we do not collect any data, the App naturally complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

---

## GDPR Compliance

This App is designed to comply with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Data Minimization**: We do not collect any personal data
- **Right to Information**: This Privacy Policy clearly explains data handling practices
- **Right to Erasure**: Uninstalling the App deletes all local data
- **Data Portability**: Not applicable (no cloud data)
- **No Tracking**: We do not track user behavior

Since this App does not collect, process, or store any personal data on servers, most GDPR provisions are automatically satisfied.

---

## CCPA Compliance

This App complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- We do not sell personal information
- We do not collect personal information
- We do not share personal information with third parties

---

## Data Deletion

If you wish to delete all data stored by this App on your device, simply uninstall the App from your device. Upon uninstallation, all local data will be completely removed.

---

## Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to users through app updates. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed.
